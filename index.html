About the Game

DunHero is an Action Roguelike RPG with Multiplayer Mode up to 4 Players.

Select your character, Level Up, collect powerful items, Explore procedurally generated worlds. Each journey is unique. Conquer all bosses and free the world from evil.

In this game you can DESTROY most of the environment and fight ANY character (even friendly NPCs).
